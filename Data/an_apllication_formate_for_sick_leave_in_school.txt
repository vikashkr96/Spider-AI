Here's a sample application format for sick leave in school:

**Application for Sick Leave**

**Student's Name:** [Your Name]
**Class/Section:** [Your Class/Section]
**Roll Number:** [Your Roll Number]
**Date:** [Current Date]

Respected [Teacher's Name/Principal's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am unwell and unable to attend school from [Date of Absence] to [Date of Return]. I am suffering from [Nature of Illness, e.g. fever, headache, etc.] and am currently under medical treatment.

I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and recommends rest for [Number of Days] days. I will make sure to catch up on any missed schoolwork and assignments as soon as I am feeling better.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding in this matter. Please grant me leave for the above-mentioned period.

Thank you for your support and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Enclosures:** Medical Certificate (if required)

**Note:** Please modify the application as per your school's requirements and policies. Also, make sure to get the application signed by your parents/guardians if required by your school.
