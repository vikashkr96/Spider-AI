Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am currently unwell and unable to attend work. Due to my illness, I am requesting a sick leave from [start date] to [end date]. I am providing this application to request your approval for my absence from work during this period.

I am suffering from [mention the illness or medical condition, e.g., fever, flu, or any other medical condition]. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and undergo treatment for the next [number of days] days. I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor to support my application.

I apologize for any inconvenience my absence may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return to the office. I will keep you updated on my status and provide any necessary documentation to support my application.

Please let me know if there are any additional requirements or documentation needed to process my application. I appreciate your understanding and support during this time.

Thank you for considering my application.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Enclosure: Medical Certificate (if applicable)

Note: You can modify this application to fit your specific needs and circumstances. Remember to include the necessary details, such as the dates of your leave, the reason for your absence, and any supporting documentation.
